Pizza

Pizza (italian:[pittsa],Napolitan[pittsa]) is a savory dish of italian origin consisting of a usally round.flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes,cheese,and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies,mushrooms,onions,olives,pineapple,meat,etc),which is then baxed at a high temperature,traditionally in a wood-fired oven.[1] A small pizza is sometimmes called a pizzetta.A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo
